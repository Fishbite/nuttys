Special Fried Rice

So who fancies Chinese? Special fried rice A classic chinese takeaway. I love this dish as a meal in itself. It's an deal way to use up left over Roast pork or you could switch for chicken from a sunday dinner. And it makes a very delicious meal by itself. The way to manage this as with every chinese dish is - everything prepared, chopped & have the mess cleared away. If you love that salty sweet & I want more taste well this is it with out an additive in sight Nutty style.

serves 4

  • 1 Tbsp Sriracha hot chilli sauce
  • 300g roasted shoulder of pork
  • 1 cup water (twice the amount of rice)
  • 4 Tbsp veg oil
  • 110g (1/2 cup) long grain rice
  • 300g chicken thighs diced
  • 120g finely diced white onions
  • 1 red chilli diced
  • 90g diced mushrooms
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • mixed bell peppers diced
  • 1/2 cup sweetcorn
  • 120g cooked peeled prawns

ingredients for the beansprout topping

  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 140g carrot grated
  • 120g very finely sliced onion
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp light soya sauce
  • 1 tsp dark soya sauce
  • 300g beansprouts

Method

Now as with most Chinese food, this is going to be a fast cook, and precision cutting is required. If everything is not cut well it will not cook evenly. So I suggest you do all your prep' before you start cooking. I even chop / dice everything well in advance before I even begin cook - sometimes a day in advance - put into pots and place in the fridge — a winner on the day after . I know I'm repeating myselfs

Method for the rice

  1. Soak & rinse your rice at least twice, once drained set aside in the fridge to dry
  2. prepare everything else chopped diced etc.
  3. set your cooked pork into the siracha sauce & the water allow to soak
  4. set a heavy based pan to heat to hot
  5. add oil & allow it to come to a shimmering heat
  6. now add in your dry rice allow to crisp slightly
  7. push the rice to one side & add your diced chicken thighs
  8. add your diced onions & stir fry until the chicken is sealed & the onions are soft
  9. add 1 red chilli stir fry
  10. add your mushrooms stir fry
  11. add in your salt & pepper
  12. add in your Sririacha hot chilli sauce & pork stir fry
  13. add your peppers sweetcorn & prawns & stir fry
  14. leave the rice to cook for 10 minutes test to check it is soft
  15. then rest in a serving dish as you do you beansproats dish

Method for the beansprout topping

  1. set your heavy based pan to heat to hot
  2. add oil allow it to come to a shimmering heat
  3. add your carrots & stir-fry
  4. add all your seasoning & stir-fry
  5. add your onions & stir-fry
  6. lastly add your beansproats just until they whilt & serve
