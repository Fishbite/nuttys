Chapti Flat Bread Recipe

Every curry benefits from a flat bread of one kind or another, but chaptis are quick and easy to make. If I call at my friends just before meal time, I'll often find the kids having fun mixing the dough in a great big mixing bowl. Here I give you a mini recipe, just enough for two! They're great with currys that have loads of gravy and even better with dry dishes such as my lamb pea and mint curry check that out here...
