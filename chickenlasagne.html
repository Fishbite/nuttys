Chicken & Mushroom Lasagne

I'm not kidding here, this dish takes a little while to construct! If I could give you one great tip: I often make the pasta sauce a day or two ahead - it only benefits from being in the fridge allowing the flavours to develop. You can also make the cheese sauce in advance and then it's not such a huge task to put this beautiful meal together.

ingredients

Here's what you need :-)

serves 6

    for the pasta sauce

  • 3 Tblsp oil
  • 3 medium onions sliced
  • 3 carrots grated
  • 3 celery stalks finely chopped
  • 5 cloves of garlic finely chopped
  • 1 pack smoked bacon lardons or 3 rashers sliced and diced
  • 450g chicken boneless thighs diced into eight
  • 8 chestnut mushrooms quartered
  • 1 400g tin plum tomatoes pulped in a blender or mashed with a potato masher
  • 2 Tbls tomatoe puree
  • 2 tsp italian mixed herbs
  • 1 tsp oragano
  • 1 bunch of fresh marjoram
  • 1 OXO™ chicken stock cube crumbled
  • 1 OXO™ vegetable stock cube crumbled
  • 1 Tbls Worchestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 x tsp black pepper
  • 400ml water

    • for the cheese sauce

  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • Pinch of Salt & black pepper
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp English mustard powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 OXO vegetable stock cube
  • 150g extra mature (No. 6 strength) cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint of whole or semi-skimmed milk

    • for the lasagne

  • 1 box of lasagna sheets
  • 1 slice of half white / half brown bread - crumbed
  • 80g grated extra strong cheddar 6
  • Dash of olive oil to drizzle

method

I'm going to break the process of making this dish down into three stages:

  • Make the pasta sauce
  • Make the cheese sauce
  • Construct the lasagne

Let's kick off with the pasta sauce:

pasta sauce

  1. Heat a large wok temp 6 (or heavy bottomed pan)
  2. Add the oil & allow it to heat up
  3. Add your onions for 5 mins
  4. Add your carrots for 5 mins
  5. Add your celery and garlic for 5 mins
  6. Add salt and pepper and dried herbs
    7. softened veg
  7. Once these vegetables have reduced by 2/3rds
  8. Push aside & add your bacon lardons brown for 10 mins
    9. cook the bacon
  9. Push the bacon under the vegetables
  10. Fry your chicken pieces for 10 mins
    11. cook the chicken

    Tip - fry your chicken in 3 small batches. If you put it all in at once the temperature of the pan will drop and they will boil! Push your chicken under your vegetables and bacon

  11. Add your mushrooms stir fry for 5 minutes
    12. cook the mushrooms
  12. Combine all the ingredients in the pan
  13. Add tomato pulp & tomato puree
  14. Add stock cubes & Worchestershire sauce
  15. Add water & bring to a rapid simmer
    16. add tomatoes & remaining ingredients
  16. Cover with a well fitting lid
  17. Turn down to med heat for 15 mins
  18. Remove the lid turn to med-high heat & allow to reduce
    19. chicken & mushroom pasta sauce
  19. Lastly, sprinkle in your marajoram

Set the pasta sauce aside to cool, popping it in the fridge overnight is best. It will let those flavours develop beautifully

Now let's tackle the cheese sauce:

cheese sauce

  1. Melt the butter in a small - med pan
  2. Add all the flour
  3. Add the onion powder with the English & Dyjon mustard
  4. Stir with a whisk or spoon to make a roux
    5. make the roux
  5. Add a little milk at a time while whisking

    6. Don't worry about the lumps, keep stirring and whisking as you add the milk and they will melt into the sauce

  6. Stir continuously to form a thick white sauce
    7. white sauce
  7. When thickened, it should coat the back of a spoon
  8. Turn the heat off
  9. Add the cheese
    10. grated cheese
  10. Stir until the cheese has melted
    11. cheese sauce

    Cover and set aside until needed.

Now let's get down to the business of putting the lasagne together

construct the lasagne

You'll need a 22.5cm square by 7.5cm deep oven-proof dish, or 3 loaf tins (or disposable dishes) with each one being enough for two people.

  1. Pre heat oven to 190c
  2. Mix the cheese and bread together
    3. breadcrumbs & cheese
  3. Put half the pasta sauce in the bottom of the dish
  4. Then top with lasagna sheets
    5. layering pasta
    layering pasta
  5. Spread half the cheese sauce onto the pasta sheets
    6. cover with cheese sauce
  6. Top with lasagna sheets
    7. layering pasta
  7. Put other half the pasta sauce on top
    8. layering pasta
    layering pasta
  8. Then top with lasagna sheets
    9. layering pasta
  9. Then put other half the cheese sauce on top
    10. top with cheese sauce
  10. Top with breadcrumb & cheese mix
  11. Add extra cheese if you like!
    12. top with cheese sauce
  12. Drizzle some olive oil over the top
  13. Cover with pre-oiled tin foil
  14. Bake in pre-heated oven at 190c for 30mins
  15. Remove the tin foil & bake for another 15mins
  16. Check it is hot in the center & browned and bubbling
    17. just out of the oven
    just out of the oven
