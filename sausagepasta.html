Sausage Pasta

Here I make another crowd pleaser using our humble ingredient the sausage. I'm not too fond of pasta dishes that are mainly pasta coated in a little sauce. I like the sauce to be the star of the dish, with the pasta adding a beautiful silky texture that has soaked up all the flavours of the sauce, but not the entire sauce

ingredients

Here's what you need :¬)

  • 1 x white & red onion sliced in food mixer
  • 2 x carrots grated in food mixer
  • 2 x sticks of celery sliced in food mixer
  • 8 x sausages skinned and cut into 3 pieces
  • 2 Tblsp tomato puree
  • 1 x 440ml tin tomatoes - blended in food mixer
  • A dash of Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs
  • 1 x 440ml tin baked beans
  • 100g penne pasta or similar (fusili etc.)
  • 1/2 red, yellow & green bell pepper
  • Fresh or dried Marjoram leaves
  • 1 veg stock, 1 chicken stock cube
  • 1 tsp black pepper corns
  • Salt

method

We'll start of by preparing the pasta, however, you can do this while cooking the main ingredients, waiting for the bell peppers to soften, for example

    Prepare the pasta:

  1. Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil
    2. salted water
  2. Add the pasta & bring back to a boil
    3. pasta in pan
  3. Cook until pasta is just cooked
  4. Drain the pasta well, reserve a little of the cooking water
    5. drain the pasta
  5. Place the pasta in cold water to stop it cooking any further
  6. Cover & set aside until needed

    Cook the main ingredients:

    Now we'll prepare and cook the main ingredients of the dish

  1. Brown the sausage in a heavy pan
    2. brown sausages
  2. Add sliced onions & cook until soft
    3. add onions
  3. Add grated carrots &cook until soft
    4. add carrots
  4. Add grated celery & cook until soft
    5. add celery
  5. Add tomato puree, ground pepper & stock cubes. Cook for a minute or two
    6. add tomato puree, ground pepper & stock cubes
  6. Add blended tomatoes & stir
    7. blended tomatoes added
  7. Add a dash of Worcestershire Sauce & the mixed herbs
    8. mixed herbs & Worcestershire Sauce
  8. Add the tin of baked beans & stir the pan
    9. add baked beans
  9. Add the sliced bell peppers, stir then cover the pan
    10. add sliced peppers
  10. Simmer until peppers are soft to your liking
  11. Add the cooked pasta & a little pasta water, about 2 Tblsp
    12. add pasta & 2 Tblsp pasta cooking water
  12. Stir well, cover & allow to simmer for 2 mins
  13. Sprinkle with the fresh Marjoram leaves to serve
    14. sausage pasta Ready to Serve :¬)
