Cheesecake

Click the Method tab below to see a step-by-step guide on how to make Cheesecake along with some pictures as a guide.

ingredients

Here's what you need :¬)

serves 8

Cake ingredients

  • 250g digestive biscuits - crushed to a fine crumb
  • 100g butter
  • 50g icing sugar
  • 400g mascarpone cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 400g cream cheese

Topping ingredients

  • 300g blue berries
  • 70g granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 165g water
  • 4 teaspoons cornstarch

* the topping is optional as a pie filling or fresh fruit would also be perfect or simply serve as it is naked.

method

You will need a small pan to heat your butter for the base and a pre-lined 23cm loose bottomed cake tin. See below.

A blender is handy to crush the biscuits but not essential as this can easily be done with a bag & a rolling pin especially if you need to vent some frustration!

NOTE: Greese the inside of the cake tin lightly with butter!

  1. Heat a small saucepan with 100g of butter till just melted & add your 250g crushed biscuits combine well
  1. Add your biscuit crumbs to the melted butter.
  1. Mix the crumbs & butter well
  1. Press the biscuit mix into a lined tin & chill
  1. Sieve the 50g icing sugar into a large bowl add 400g mascarpone cream & mix well
  2. Add 1 tsp vanilla extract & the 400g cream cheese & combine well
  1. Once your biscuit base has completely cooled you can now top with your cream mixture smoothing the top with a pallet knife
  1. make a lip around the edge to hold the topping
  1. Add the topping
  1. Place into the fridge until ready to serve

I have topped mine with a blueberry fruit compote alternatively use a tin of Princess™ Fillings & Toppings — Black Cherry which is a fast and easy pie topping or simply top with fresh berries of your choosing.
