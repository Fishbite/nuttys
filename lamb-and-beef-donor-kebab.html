Lamb and Beef Donor Kebab

So now we are talking the ultimate Home-Away! And I am truely excited about this recipe Lamb and Beef Donor Kebab. Now I do own a Rotisere spit but decided that would simply be too cruel! As 90% do not have a spit, so it was time for a bitta research. In true Nutty style "By gove I think I have sussed it". All you need is baking parchment and an oven, grill or even air fryer. We are going to wrap and bake @ 200c for for 15 to 18 minutes. So this is faster than a phone call and a guy on a push bike every day of the week.

It is a mixture of the two meats, but you can just use Lamb or Beef if prefered. The actual method or cooking does not change.

Click the Method tab below to see a step-by-step guide on how to make Lamb and Beef Donor Kebab along with some pictures as a guide.

ingredients

Here's what you need :¬)

serves 4 to 6

  • 500g Beef mince
  • 475g Lamb mince
  • 2 medium onions grated
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 80 ml
  • 3 tsp cumin ground
  • 2.5 tsp corriander ground
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper ground
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • *Baking parchment to cook in

Extras To serve with
Lamb and Beef Donor Kebab

  • Pitta breads toasted
  • Iceberg Lettuce torn
  • Cucumber finely sliced
  • Tomatoes finely sliced
  • Raw white onion finely sliced
  • Coleslaw
  • Garlic mayonnaise
  • Hot chilli sauce / mayonnaise

All optional, a bowl to dish up in will suffice!

method

So firstly the mince will need to be ground so food processor it is or a nice pounding with your fists. This is add everything listed below to the mea you are using and combine well. Then roll out inbetween two sheets of baking parchment and bake @ 200c for 15 to 18 minutes. I hear you is it really that easy? Well frankly yes :-)
