Nutty's Super Scrummy Sausage Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack sausages, skinned & cut into 3 pieces
  • 2 red onions, sliced
  • 2 medium carrots, grated
  • 2 sticks of celery, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 440ml of tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 440ml of tinned baked beans
  • 1 veg stock cube
  • 1 - 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 red, yellow & green pepper, sliced
  • salt & pepper for seasoning
  • sprinkle of fresh marjoram
  • 100g fusilli pasta (or pasta of choice)
veg prep

Method:

  1. pre-heat large pan / wok
  2. add sausage to the pan & brown all over
    3. brown suasages
  3. add onions, fry for 5mins
  4. add corrots, fry for 5mins
  5. add celery & garlic, fry for 5mins
    6. add veg
  6. add mixed herbs & oregano
  7. crumble in veg stock cube
  8. add tomato puree & worcestershire suace
  9. stir!
  10. add tomotoes, stir & combine well
  11. add beans, stir gently
  12. cover & simmer for about 10mins
  13. add sliced peppers, cover & simmer for 5mins, or, until they are as soft as you like
    14. add peppers
  14. add cooked pasta & stir well
  15. sprinkle with fresh marjoram to serve
    16. sausage casserole ready to serve
