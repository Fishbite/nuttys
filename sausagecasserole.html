Nutty's Super Scrummy Sausage & Bean Casserole

Love sausage & baked beans? Trust me, you're gonna love this one! It's a warming home-style casserole with loads of sauce. Serve simply with a fresh crusty buttered baguette, or, make some creamy mashed potato for a cold winter's night feast

ingredients

Here's what you need :¬)

serves 4 - 6

  • 1 pack sausages, skinned & cut into 3 pieces
  • 2 red onions, sliced
  • 2 medium carrots, grated
  • 2 sticks of celery, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 440ml of tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 440ml of tinned baked beans
  • 1 veg stock cube
  • 1 - 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 red, yellow & green pepper, sliced
  • salt & pepper for seasoning
  • sprinkle of fresh marjoram

method

  1. Pre-heat large pan / wok
  2. Add sausage to the pan & brown all over
    3. brown sausages
  3. Add onions, fry for 5mins
  4. Add carrots, fry for 5mins
  5. Add celery & garlic, fry for 5mins
    6. add veg
  6. Add salt, pepper, mixed herbs & oregano
  7. Crumble in veg stock cube
  8. Add tomato puree, worcestershire sauce, tomatoes
  9. Add beans & stir gently
  10. Cover & simmer for about 10mins
  11. Add sliced peppers
  12. Cover and simmer until peppers are soft as you like
    13. add peppers
  13. Sprinkle with fresh marjoram or thyme to serve
    14. sausage casserole serve with warm buttered baguette or mash
