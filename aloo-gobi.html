Aloo Gobi

So here's a my version of Aloo Gobi — a very basic cauliflower & potato curry or side dish. I like to eat mine with a fork outta the pan as I'm testing it — trust me it's that good! Breakfast with a fried egg the next day, yeah! Side to an Indian curry, the choice is yours. A dish in itself and it does contain a fair amount of spices! I like to use a salad style new potato as they'll stay firm and not lose their identity amongst the cauliflower & onions ect. So, if you'd like a fairly quick vegetarian dinner, try this. It's a dry-style stir fry, not truly a curry, but a dish that allows the spices to truly sing.

method

Now as always get your "BIG PANTS" and your ingriedients ready, pots for stuff, ya know the drill! Once we are cooking we want to nuture our pan, not hunt in the back of the cupboard for what we are missing! I use my trusty wok for this — as it gets hot and can handle the amount of vegetables allowing them to crisp up. Try not to use a small pan the result will be MUSH.

Firstly, get a pan large enough to fit both your cauliflower & potatoes to par boil

  1. Warm your whole spices (except cumin seeds) in a small pan & allow to cool
  2. Grind your salt, peppercorns, methi & corriander seeds & add your spice powders to the mix
  3. Get everything chopped, measured ready to go
  4. Fill your large pan with water & 1 tsp salt & sliced potatoes
  5. Bring to the boil and simmer for 15 mins
  6. Go do some dusting or have tea!
  7. 15 minutes later add your cauliflower for 5 minutes
  8. Drain immediately they are aldente
  9. Immerse in cold water to stop the cooking process & get them truely cold
  10. Separate the potatoes & cauli'
  11. Heat a large heavy pan, add oil and your cumin seeds
  12. Once sizzling add your onions & brown lightly
  13. Add your spice mix now
  14. Add your garlic slices
  15. Move the garlic & spices to the side & turn the heat to max
  1. No running off now! Get the potaoes in and occassionally stir so they get really crispy bits
  2. Once they are all partially fried add your cauliflower repeat the stir frying
