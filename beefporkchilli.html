serves 6 to 8

  • 1 medium white onions sliced
  • 1 medium red onion sliced
  • 2 carrots grated
  • 2 celery stalks finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic finely chopped
  • 450g beef and pork mince
  • 1 400g tin plum tomatoes mashed
  • 1 400g tin red kidney beans and all the fluid
  • 8 chestnut mushrooms quartered - optional
  • 2 Tblsp tomato puree
  • 1 OXO™ beef stock cube crumbled
  • 1 OXO™ vegetable stock cube crumbled
  • 1 tsp Worchestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1.5 tsp extra hot chilli powder
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chilli powder - optional but it does add colour
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 red green and yellow pepper cut into strips then in half
  • 3 fresh green chilli's
  • 400ml water

  1. Heat a large wok to med-high ( or wide bottomed pan )
  2. Add a 3rd of the mince broken into strips

    3. Tip - fry your mince in 3 small batches. If you put it all in at once the temperature of the pan will drop and they will boil!

  3. Push to the side of the pan when brown
  4. Add some more mince & brown
  5. repeat
    6. brown the meat
  6. Set the meat aside on a plate
  7. Add the cumin seeds to the hot pan
  8. Fry for a minute until they start to smell

    9. Tip - The vegetables need softening not browning as such so set temp to med-hot

  9. Add your onions for 5 mins
  10. Add your carrots for 5 mins
  11. Add your celery and garlic for 5 mins
  12. Add salt and pepper and dried herbs
  13. Reduce veg by 2 / 3rds in size
  14. Push aside, add the mince & juices back into the pan
  15. Stir fry until all ingredients are combined
  16. Add your chilli powder and cumin powder
  17. Stir fry for 10mins
  18. Push everything to the side again
  19. Add mushrooms, fry for 5 mins until soft
  20. Stir to combine all the ingredients in the pan
    21. meat and veg combined
  21. Add your tomatoes, tomato puree, stock cubes
  22. Add a dash of worchestershire sauce
    23. bring to rapid simmer
  23. Bring to a rapid simmer
  24. Add sliced peppers
    25. add sliced peppers
  25. Cover with a tight fitting lid
  26. Simmer gently on low-mew heat for 15mins
  27. Remove lid, turn heat up to med
  28. Reduce the sauce
  29. Sprinkle with marjoram & fresh chilli slices to serve
