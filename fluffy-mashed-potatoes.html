Fluffy Mashed Potatoes

Now who does not love a well made, well seasoned mash potatoes Fluffy mashed potatoes These can add so much pleasure to any dinner whether you top your dish off and bake it or simple serve it along side your fish or meat. A winner @ Nutty's place

serves 3 -4

  • 900 g potatoes, peeled & quartered
  • 1 heaped Tbsp Salt
  • 50ml warm milk
  • 50g warm butter

method

Use a larger pan than required so you are not at risk of the water boiling over. Completely cover the rinsed potatoes and add a good tablespoon of salt. Once boiling, reduce the heat slightly and set a timer for 20 minutes.

  1. rinse the potatoes in a large pan to remove some of the starch
  2. cover with cold water
  3. add 1 good heaped Tbsp salt
  4. cover and bring to the boil
  5. simmer for 20mins, or until soft enough to stick a fork in them without any resistance
  6. drain through a colander
  7. put the milk & butter into the potato pan
  8. add the potatoes back into the pan
  9. cover and allow them to steam for a couple of mins while the butter melts
  10. mash with a potato masher until they are all broken down finish by whisking the mash vigorously with a fork until it is completely lump free*

*Finishing the mash with a fork stops it from going 'gloopy' and starchy (as it can do with a potato masher) giving you the perfect fluffy mash.
