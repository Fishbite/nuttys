Spring Onion Chicken

As the name suggests the onions are key try not to buy super market miniture bunches you need long asian style Spring onions this is sweet & savoury dish packed with spring onions Spring Onion Chicken should take you about 20 miniutes to cook. I am using thighs as they are so flavoursome if using breast reduce the cooking time. 10 ingriedients in total this is so easy I'm almost embarressed to share it with you! :-)

Serves 4

  • 1 Tbls honey
  • 2 1/2 Tbls light soya sauce
  • 3 Tbls Oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black peppercorns ground
  • 1 1/2 tsp cornflour
  • 4 lrg chicken thighs boned skinned & diced
  • 4 large spring onions diced*
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3 cm piece of fresh ginger
  • 2 Tbls vegetable oil
  • 2 Tbls shaoxing rice wine vinegar
  • fresh lime quatered optional

*Slice to your liking diaginally at an angle for the whites and a fine dice for the greens. Keep both separately white & greens as we will add the whites with the chicken & the greens at the end of the cooking process

method

First I would advise getting some rinced basmati rice onto cook of course you could do this the day before then make my "Egg fried rice" which can be found in accompanyments. Then mix your marinade. I am doing the marinade in my new stick blender pot as it new and at the moment very exciting (Sure it will end up lost in the back of a cupboard eventually) but who does not love a new gadget. As I said this is a fast stir fry dish so we need to be organised & ready with everything measured & prepared. I am using a wok but you could use a large frying pan if you do not have a wok. Serve with rice and lime wedges if desired.

  1. Cut the chicken down into nice bite sized pieces
  2. get a suitable plastic container and mix your marinade
  3. 1 Tbls honey 2 1/2 Tbls light soya sauce 3 Tbls Oyster sauce 1/2 tsp black peppercorns ground 1 1/2 tsp cornflour
  4. add 1 spring onion garlic & ginger & blitz to combine and chop finely
  5. mix the chicken thighs throughly into the marinade
  6. set the marinaded chicken aside for 5 minutes
  7. slice your spring onions & separate white & greens
  8. Heat a heavy based pan to high once hot add 2 Tbls oil
  9. once the oil shimmers turn the ring down add the marinaded chicken
  10. now add the white spring onions
  11. stir fry for 4 to 5 minutes add 2 Tbls shaoxing rice wine vinegar
  12. add in the green spring onions stir fry for another 1 to 2 minutes
  13. Quarter a small lime & add to the side of each serving
