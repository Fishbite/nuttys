# Nutty’s Kitchen – Reel Creation Cheat Sheet

## 1. Hook Fast

Start with **hero dish image**

Text stack:

Nutty’s Kitchen  
Super Special  
Recipe Name  
Big Flavour • Tiny Budget

---

## 2. Transition

Micro pause → Arc tagline

Example:

_Slightly Nutty_

First beat hits here.

---

## 3. Show Motion

Always prioritise:

Action → Ingredients → Cooking

Avoid too many still shots.

---

## 4. Edit to Beats

Major beat → Scene change  
Minor beat → Ingredient action  
Micro beat → Stir / tap / movement

Natural feel:

Beat → Action (slightly after)

---

## 5. Keep Text Minimal

Max **3–4 words**

Examples:

Garlic • Ginger • Turmeric  
Now the magic…  
Hot oil • Big flavour

---

## 6. Strong Ending

Show finished dish.

One line only:

Full recipe  
NuttysKitchen.co.uk

---

## 7. Loop the Reel

End frame ≈ Start frame.

Encourages **automatic replay**.

---

## 8. Caption Formula

Search phrase  
Hook  
Tip  
Recipe link  
Question  
10–12 hashtags

---

## Golden Rule

Hook → Rhythm → Food → Curiosity → Recipe click
