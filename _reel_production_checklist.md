# Nutty’s Kitchen – Instagram Reel Production Checklist

## 1. Define the Goal

- Target length: **25–35 seconds**
- Reel should **hook viewers and drive traffic to the full recipe**
- Focus on **visual satisfaction**, not full instruction

---

## 2. Opening Hook (Hero Frame)

Start with a strong image of the finished dish.

**Text Stack Structure**

Nutty’s Kitchen  
Super Special  
Recipe Name  
Big Flavour • Tiny Budget

Guidelines:

- Clean layout
- Readable text
- Strong visual contrast

---

## 3. Arc Tagline Transition

After the intro stack:

- Insert a **micro pause**
- Replace stack with **arc tagline**

Example:

_Slightly Nutty_

The first **music beat should land when the arc text completes**.

---

## 4. Cooking Sequence Structure

Prioritise **motion over still images**.

Preferred order:

1. Action shot (ingredients added)
2. Ingredient reference shot
3. Cooking progression

Avoid static shots unless they **add clarity**.

---

## 5. Music & Beat Synchronisation

Use CapCut beat detection.

Workflow:

Track 1 → Major beats  
Track 2 → Minor beats

Editing rhythm:

Major beat → Scene change  
Minor beat → Ingredient action  
Micro beat → Taps / stirring / movement

Natural timing:

Beat → Action (slightly after)

Occasional emphasis:

Action → Beat

---

## 6. Maintain Visual Rhythm

Avoid:

- Long static holds
- Chaotic rapid cuts

Good pacing pattern:

Action  
Action  
Action  
Micro pause  
Action

---

## 7. On-Screen Text Rules

Keep captions short.

**3–4 words maximum**

Examples:

Garlic • Ginger • Turmeric  
Now the magic…  
Hot oil • Big flavour

Use bullet symbol **•** for visual clarity.

---

## 8. Final Hero Shot

Show the finished dish clearly.

Avoid repeating the full intro stack.

Use a simple call-to-action:

Full recipe  
NuttysKitchen.co.uk

Let the **food remain the hero**.

---

## 9. Seamless Reel Loop

Design ending to visually connect to the opening frame.

Example:

Hero dish → Loop → Opening hero shot

This increases **watch time and replays**.

---

## 10. Caption Structure

1. Search phrase (first line)
2. Hook
3. Short description or tip
4. Recipe link
5. Engagement question
6. Hashtags

Example:

Tarka dhal recipe with sizzling onion and chilli tarka.

Big flavour • Tiny budget.

Full recipe: NuttysKitchen.co.uk

Do you like your dhal mild or fiery?

---

## 11. Hashtag Strategy

Use **10–12 focused hashtags**.

Example mix:

#TarkaDhal  
#DhalRecipe  
#IndianHomeCooking  
#LentilRecipes  
#BudgetCooking  
#CheapMeals  
#VegetarianDinner  
#MeatFreeMeals  
#ComfortFoodRecipes  
#HomeCookingUK  
#NuttysKitchenUK

Always include **#NuttysKitchenUK**.

---

## Core Philosophy

Strong opening  
Clean rhythm  
Minimal text  
Beautiful food  
Clear call to action

The reel should **spark curiosity**, not teach the full recipe.
