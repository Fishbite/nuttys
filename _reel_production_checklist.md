# Nutty’s Kitchen – Instagram Reel Production Checklist

## 1. Define the Goal

☐ Target length: **25–35 seconds**
☐ Reel should **hook viewers and drive traffic to the full recipe**
☐ Focus on **visual satisfaction**, not full instruction

---

## 2. Opening Hook (Hero Frame)

☐ Start with **strong hero image of the finished dish**
☐ Display intro **text stack**

Example structure:

Nutty’s Kitchen
Super Special
Recipe Name
Big Flavour • Tiny Budget

☐ Ensure text is **clear, balanced and readable**

---

## 3. Arc Tagline Transition

☐ Insert **micro pause** after text stack
☐ Replace stack with short **arc text**

Example:

“Slightly Nutty”

☐ First **music beat hits as arc text finishes**

---

## 4. Cooking Sequence Structure

Prioritise **motion over still images**

Preferred order:

☐ Action shot (ingredients added)
☐ Ingredient reference shot
☐ Cooking progression

Avoid unnecessary static shots unless they **clarify ingredients**.

---

## 5. Music & Beat Synchronisation

☐ Import music
☐ Use CapCut **beat detection**

Workflow:

Track 1 → Major beats
Track 2 → Minor beats

Editing rhythm:

Major beat → scene change
Minor beat → ingredient action
Micro beat → taps / stirring / motion

Natural timing:

Beat → Action (slightly after)

Occasional emphasis:

Action → Beat

---

## 6. Maintain Visual Rhythm

Avoid:

☐ Long static holds
☐ Rapid confusing cuts

Aim for rhythm like:

Action
Action
Action
Micro pause
Action

---

## 7. On-Screen Text Rules

☐ Keep captions **3–4 words max**

Examples:

Garlic • Ginger • Turmeric
Now the magic…
Hot oil • Big flavour

Use bullets **•** to keep text visually clean.

---

## 8. Final Hero Shot

☐ Show the finished dish clearly
☐ Avoid repeating the full intro stack

Use **one short call-to-action**

Example:

Full recipe
NuttysKitchen.co.uk

Let the **food be the focus**.

---

## 9. Seamless Reel Loop

☐ Ending visually matches opening shot

Example:

Hero dish → loop → opening hero image

This encourages **automatic replay and longer watch time**.

---

## 10. Caption Structure

1️⃣ Search phrase first line
2️⃣ Hook
3️⃣ Short description or tip
4️⃣ Recipe link
5️⃣ Engagement question
6️⃣ 10–12 targeted hashtags

Example structure:

Tarka dhal recipe with sizzling onion and chilli tarka.

Big flavour • Tiny budget.

Full recipe: NuttysKitchen.co.uk

Do you like your dhal mild or fiery?

---

## 11. Hashtag Strategy

Use **10–12 focused hashtags**

Example mix:

#TarkaDhal
#DhalRecipe
#IndianHomeCooking
#LentilRecipes
#BudgetCooking
#CheapMeals
#VegetarianDinner
#MeatFreeMeals
#ComfortFoodRecipes
#HomeCookingUK
#NuttysKitchenUK

Always include **#NuttysKitchenUK**.

---

## Core Reel Philosophy

Strong opening
Clean rhythm
Minimal text
Beautiful food
Clear call to action

The reel should **spark curiosity**, not teach the full recipe.
