Chicken Chilli Mexican

The chorizo is the star here. You also have protein from the beans, so smaller chicken thighs are fine to use, they'll still add the depth of chicken flavour to the dish.

I have to say, this dish has a bit of a chilli kick!!! So, if you want to create a slightly milder version, omit the green chillis and use a mild chilli powder, if needs be, but, we like a bit of a punch!

Serve with tomato rice, or if you are in a rush, use boneless chicken thighs and pop the cooked chilli and tomato rice in a warm torilla wrap.

ingredients

Here's what you need, but, as I said, if you want to create a milder, child friendly meal, omit the green chillis and use a mild chilli powder

  • 1 large white onion
  • 1large red onion
  • Chorizo sausage sliced into 5mm thick discs
  • 1 400g tin plum tomatoes
  • 1 400g tin Cannellini beans drained & washed
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp cumin whole
  • 1 tsp extra hot chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper corns
  • 1 Tblsp garlic pulp
  • 1 Tblsp tomato puree
  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 1 400ml tin water
  • 3 bell peppers (red, yellow & green) 15mm slices halved
  • Sprinkle of corriander for garnish
  • 2 small green chillis (Indian)
