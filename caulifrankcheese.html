cauliflower cheese & frankfurter bake

This dish could easily be totally vegetarian just use vegetarian frankfurters (yummy) Serve with a warm crusty bagette with lashings of butter and a dash of worchester sauce if it takes your fancy. I buy bake at home bagettes as life is to short to make one :¬) If you like this dish please try Cauliflower cheese & beans bake.

ingredients

Here's what you need :¬)

serves 4

    cheese sauce

  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • Pinch of salt & black pepper
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp English mustard powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 OXO™ vegetable stock cube
  • 150g extra strong (6) cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint of whole milk

    • roasting dish

  • 1 large cauliflower broken into florettes
  • 1 Tblsp salt
  • Fresh cauliflower leaves
  • Pack of 10 frankfurters
  • 3 small cherry tomatoes cut into half
  • 1 tin baked beans
  • 1 slice of bread crumbed

method

To prep the cauli', cut the florettes in half or quarter to make sure they cook evenly. The stalks of the florettes and leaves are quite fiberous and therefore, don't cook quite as easily as the florettes themselves. You can also run a sharp knife down the middle of the stalks on the leaves if you wish. Once the cauli' is done, we can move onto the cheese sauce before finally building the final dish.

    prep the cauli

  1. Cut the cauli' florettes into even size pieces
  2. Place into a large pan of salted (1 Tblsp) water
  3. Bring to the boil & simmer rapidly for 5 mins
  4. Add the cauliflower leaves for a minute or so
  5. Drain & immerse in ice-cold water
  6. Drain well when stone cold

    7. make the cheese sauce

  7. Melt the butter in a pan on low to med heat
  8. Add stock cube, flour, mustard & onion powder
  9. Stir to form a roux
  10. Add a little milk slowly while whisking
  11. Add more milk gradually while whisking
  12. Whisk continuously as it thickens*

    13. *you should end up with a thick smooth sauce that clings nicely to the back a spoon

  13. Turn off the heat and add 2/3rds of the cheese
  14. Mix the rest of the cheese with the breadcrumbs
    15. cheese & breadcrumbs

    prep the roasting dish

  15. Tip the beans into the baking dish
  16. Place the frankfuters on top
    17. beans & frankfurters
  17. Arrange the cauli' on the frankfurters
    18. add cooked cauliflower
  18. Pour the cheese sauce over the top
    19. add cheese sauce
  19. Place the cauli leaves on top
  20. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs & cheese on top
    21. cheese & breadcrumbs
  21. Top with the cherry tomato halves
    22. add tomatoes
  22. Give the tomatoes a twist of salt & pepper
  23. Roast in the oven at 180c for about 45 mins
    24. ready to serve

    Cooking time & temp: Roast in the oven @ 180c for about 45 minutes, or until the top is nicely toasted and the center hot. A sharp knife should easily go through the cauliflower stem with ease. The reason the timing can not be exact is every cauliflower is different, some have a more open florettes and no two are exact in size / weight

    ...and plated!
