Sausage & Pepper Pie

A beautifully savoury mash covered family pleaser in a lovely rich sauce with sausage, onions & peppers

ingredients

Here's what you need :Â¬)

  • 1 pack sausages, skinned & cut into 3 pieces
  • 1 white onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 440ml of tomato soup (cheapest you can buy will do!)
  • 1 veg stock cube
  • 1 - 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 red, yellow & green pepper, 2.5cm cubes
  • salt & pepper for seasoning
  • mashed potato to finish

method

The ingredients of this are very similar to the sausage & pepper casserole except here we use a tin of tomato soup instead of beans and tomatoes. We also keep the peppers a little crispier because we'll finish the dish in the oven to give the mash a wonderful golden crispy toasted finish.

  1. pre-heat large pan / wok
  2. add sausage to the pan & brown all over
    3. brown sausages
  3. add onions, fry for 5mins
  4. add salt, pepper, mixed herbs & oregano
  5. crumble in veg stock cube
  6. add tomato puree, worcestershire sauce, tomato soup then stir & combine well
  7. cover & simmer for about 10mins
  8. add cubed peppers, cover & simmer for 5mins or less so they still have a little bit of a crunch to them
    9. sausage & pepper pie mix
  9. transfer to a deep casserole dish and cover with mashed potato
    10. sausage & pepper pie covered with mash Ready to brown in the oven
