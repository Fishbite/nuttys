Cauliflower Cheese bake

with beans

Cauliflower, what a wonderful ingredient, if treated with love! Here it will be par-boiled then baked. I'm not a fan of boiled watery cauli' with a meal as I find it a little tasteless. But bake in cheese sauce, add beans, and I'll be first in the queue :¬P

To make this totally vegetarian, just leave out the bacon.

ingredients

Here's what you need :¬)

serves 4

    for the cheese sauce

  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • Pinch of Salt & black pepper
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp English mustard powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 OXO vegetable stock cube
  • 150g extra mature (No. 6 strength) cheddar cheese
  • 50g extra cheese for topping
  • 1 pint of whole or semi-skimmed milk

    for the rest of the dish

  • 1 large cauliflower, broken into florettes
  • cauliflower leaves
  • Dash of oil
  • Knob of butter
  • 3 rashers of thick smoked bacon cut into lardons
  • 1 med red onion quartered & sliced
  • 3 cloves fresh garlic sliced
  • 6 chestnut mushrooms sliced
  • 1 med tomato cut into 8 wedges
  • 1 tin baked beans

method

This dish could easily be totally vegetarian but if you like smoked bacon as it was added raw the bacon juices permiate the topping (yummy) Serve with a warm crusty bagette with lashings of butter and a dash of worchester sauce if it takes your fancy. I buy bake at home bagettes as life is to short to make one ;¬) If you try this and want another option, coming soon: 'Frankfurter Sausage Cauliflower Cheese' is a winner with the children.

  1. Put the cauli' florettes in a large pan
  2. Cover with water & add 1 Tblsp salt
  3. Bring to the boil & simmer for 5mins
  4. Add the cauliflower leaves for 1 min
  5. Drain & immerse in ice cold water
  6. Drain well when stone cold
  7. Put onions into a microwave proof dish
  8. Sprinkle with salt & a dash if oil
  9. Microwave on full power for 2 min
  10. Mix the hot onions with the mushrooms & garlic
    11. mushrooms & onions
  11. Add a knob of butter & set aside
  12. Tip the beans into an oven proof dish / tin
  13. Place the cauliflower florettes onto the beans
    14. work from the edges inward
  14. Spoon half of the cheese sauce over the top
    15. Add some cheese sauce
  15. Cover with the cauliflower leaves
    16. cover with cauli' leaves
  16. Spoon over the rest of the cheese sauce
    17. cover with the rest of the cheese sauce
  17. Cover with the onion & mushrooms
    18. add onions & mushrooms
  18. Sprinkle with bacon lardons
  19. Sprinkle the rest of the grated cheese over the top
  20. Decorate with tomato wedges
    21. add tomato wedges

    Cooking time temp: Roast in the oven @180c for about 45 minutes, or until the top is nicely toasted and the center hot. A sharp knife should easily go through the cauliflower stem with ease. The reason the timing can not be exact is every cauliflower is different, some have a more open florettes and no two are exact in size / weight
