Chinese Noodles with Beansprouts

Chinese Noodles With Beansprouts is a light and refreshing noodle dish full of fresh crunchy vegetables, lightly seasoned and flavoured with sesame oil. All the work is in the chopping of the vegetables, once this is completed all you have to do is stir fry on the hottest heat you have available. So, prepare, prepare, prepare, or you're going to burn stuffs!

Click the Method tab below to see a step by step guide on how to make my Chinese Noodles With Beansprouts with pictures.

ingredients

Here's what you need :¬)

  • 1 Tblsp veg oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 medium carrot cut into fine batons
  • 1/2 medium onion finely diced
  • 5cm fresh ginger cut into small batons
  • 2 cloves garlic cut into small batons
  • 150g ready to wok medium noodles
  • 150g beansprouts
  • 1 Tblsp cornflour
  • 1 Tblsp water
  • 2 tsp light soya sauce
  • 2 tsp dark soya sauce
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 fresh green chilli finely diced

method

To make my Chinese Noodles With Beansprouts set your heavy bottomed wok onto the fastest, hottest ring, put it on full and wait untill you can see the heat. Once your wok is up to temperature add the ingriedients to the wok in order, no wondering off to make tea here, this is 5 minute cook! So, get everything ready to go: Mix your cornflour with the water, lay your ingriedients out as listed in the ingriedients list and get ready to wok & roll!

So, here goes, on your marks, get set, WOK it!

  1. Add oil to a very hot wok
  2. Add salt, sugar & carrots - stir fry
