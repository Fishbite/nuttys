Mediterranian Style Vegetables with Pork Steaks This dish is a wonderful mix of carrots courgettes aubergine & sliced fresh garlic. You to need to do a fair amount of chopping, creating various sizes of vegetable batons, i.e. each type of veg' needs to be of a certain baton size so that everything cooks evenly. Once that is done the dish is a breeze to put together. Italian dried herbs and oregano mixed with olive oil and tomato puree create a light sauce to compliment the vegetables. Delicious sliced peppers added to the bake with seasoned pork steaks that go on top of the vegetables after partly cooking. A final summery florish of fresh marjoram will complete the dish.

Ingredients for the first bake

  • 1 large carrots cut into small batons
  • 1 large red onion cut into 12 through the core
  • 2 aubergines cut into medium batons
  • 1 large courgette cut into larger batons
  • 2 bowls water & lemon juice
  • 250g fresh baby plum tomatoes halved
  • 5 cloves garlic sliced
  • 3 Tbls olive oil
  • 1 Tbls tomatoe puree
  • 2 tsp dried italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 vegetable stock cube crumbled
  • a good grind of salt & pepper onto each layer

Please refer to the pictures as a guide for the size of your batons. This is important as the carrots will take the longest to cook, with aubergines taking the least.

Ingredients for the second bake

  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 green bell pepper
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper
  • 4 Pork shoulder steaks
  • a good grind of salt & pepper
  • dust of smoked paprika

to finish

  • fresh Marjoram

method

Well! There's a little bit of choppin' to do for this dish, so now's the time to hone those knife skills! Pay attention to the size of those vegetable batons, the carrots particularly need to be the thinnest cut so that they have a chance of cooking before the soft veg' (aubergines, courgettes) turn to mush!

Method for the first bake

  1. pre-heat your oven to 180c
  2. once chopped, place the courgettes & aubergines into lemon water or they will brown
  3. get a large ovenproof dish and create layers of aubergines, courgettes, carrots, onions tomatoes & garlic. Make sure to season each layer with salt & pepper. Disgard the lemon water
  1. in a separate bowl mix olive oil, tomato puree, dried herbs & veg stock cube
  2. mix & pour across the vegetables
  1. cover with tin foil & bake @ 180c for 30 minutes
  1. slice your peppers & add to the dish
  1. re-cover & cook for 15 minutes more

Method for second bake

  1. take your pork steaks out the fridge
  2. rub or spray oil onto eack steak
  3. season each side of the steaks with salt & pepper
  4. dust with a little smoked paprika
  1. remove your vegetables from the oven & stir together
  2. add seasoned pork steaks & return to the oven uncovered
  1. bake again @ 180 c for 20 minutes turning the steaks half way through
  2. sprinkle with fresh marjoram when serving

Serving suggestion: Mediterranian Style Vegetables with Pork Steaks A big serving spoon, plates, knives, forks and if you wish — some baby new potatoes in chive butter "Simples". Yummy!

And, what to do with the left-over veg'? Well, I pulled some more pork steaks out and put them in a marinade of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic and paprika — just enough to coat them and left them in the fridge overnight. Next day, I simply seasoned them with salt and pepper, chucked them on the grill and re-heated the left-over veg'. 4 to 5 mins and I have dinner for day two! ;Â¬)
