curry leaf masala chicken fry

Now, this is something really special—I would even go so far as to say authentic Indian food: Curry Leaf Masala Chicken Fry.

This masala has a rich, earthy flavor with a distinct curry leaf aroma. I wouldn’t class it as hot, but some say I have the palate of an elephant’s foot! Probably from growing up with fresh chilies as a snack. 😉 But I digress…

This was introduced to me by an Indian friend, and I have to say, I was thrilled when he said, You’ve made it well.

Praise indeed! I mused silently to myself.

I tend to make my spice mixes and even pastes the day before—reducing the workload when all you want to do is eat.

So, without ado, let's do this ting!

Click the Method tab below to see a step-by-step guide on how to make Curry Leaf Masala Chicken Fry along with some pictures as a guide.

ingredients

Here's what you need :¬)

serves 3 - 4

Ingredients for Curry leaf Masala

  • 30g curry leaves (stalks discarded)
  • 2 Tbsp whole coriander seeds
  • 1 1/2 tsp whole cumin seeds
  • 1 1/2 tsp whole fennel seeds
  • 5cm cinnamon stick
  • 8 whole cloves
  • 3 whole cardamom
  • 2 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 7 dried red chilli's

Ingredients for Curry Leaf Chicken Fry

  • 1 kg bone in chicken*
  • 5 tsp curry leaf masala
  • 3 Tbsp vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds whole
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds whole
  • 4 red onions finely diced
  • 4 fresh green chilli's slit
  • 3 tsp fresh ginger garlic paste**
  • 2 fresh tomatoes finely diced
  • 1 to 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • fresh curry Leaves to garnish

*Try to find the smallest thighs, or better yet, use legs. Indians usually cut through the bone—which I did attempt here, but nearly broke me wrist in the process! 😅

(My new scissors for bone-cutting are officially banished to the back of the cupboard... forever!)

**Try to make this fresh with 5 cloves garlic & 5cm piece of ginger, peel the garlic & hit with back of a wide knife. As to the ginger roughly chop it. Then put both into a pestle & mortar and bash it till fine.

method

Right are BIG PANTS a on a brief description of how to approach this dish - get your self organised prepare your curry leaf masala spices as above. Whelst they are all cooling move onto step of preparing the Chicken Fry then pop back here to complete the curry leaf masala

Method Curry leaf Masala

  1. First, prepare 50 g of curry leaves. Just check they are clean & fresh
  2. Put into a hot dry pan & dry roast the curry leaves till they change colour
  3. Once they are crispy, transfer to a plate and cool
  4. Use the same pan, add two tbsp of coriander seeds, one and half tsp of cumin seeds, one and half tsp of fennel seeds, a piece of cinnamon, 7-8 cloves, 3 cardamom, two tsp of pepper corns, 7-8 red chillies
  5. Dry roast them till you get a nice aroma, once done transfer it to the same plate and again allow them to cool.
  6. Transfer the ingredients to a spice grinder and grind it to a nice fine powder

Method Chicken fry

chop & grind all your fresh ingriedients. Then get your 1 tsp mustard & cumin seeds whole ready to dry fry. Set all your powdered spices onto a plate ready to add to the dish. We are good to go now...

  1. Take a wide heavy based wok & heat adding 3 tbsp of oil once the oil shimmers
  2. Add one tsp of mustard seeds, one & half tsp of one & half cumin seeds & fennel.
  3. 2 x large onions finely chopped, four green chillies slit and stir fry untill the aroma fills the kitchen.
  4. large onions finely chopped, four green chillies slit and stir fry untill the aroma fills the kitchen.
  5. Add 3 tsp of ginger garlic paste and mix well.
  6. Add 2 tomatoes chopped and mix again.
  7. Add one tsp of salt and one tsp of turmeric powder and mix it all in.
  8. Add one kilo chicken pieces and mix it all together.
  9. Add 2 tomatoes chopped and mix again.
  10. Close the pan with the lid and cook for 5 minutes.
  11. Add two tsp of curry leaf masala and cook well. About 10 minutes
  12. Again add two tsp of curry leaf masala and mix well.
  13. Once it is dryer, add again some curry leaf masala and mix together.
  14. Add some fresh curry leaves and turn off the stove
  15. Taste & add salt if required
  16. Curry leaf chicken fry is ready to be served nice and hot.
