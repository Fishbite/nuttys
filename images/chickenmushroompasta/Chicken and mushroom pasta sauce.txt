Chicken and mushroom italian pasta sauce

Ingredients

three tbls oil
3 x medium onions sliced
3 x carrots grated
3 x celery stalks finely chopped
5 x cloves of garlic finely chopped
I pack smoked bacon lardons or 3 rashers sliced and  diced
450g chicken boneless thighs diced into eight 
8 x chestnut mushrooms quartered
2 x tbls tomato puree
1 x 400g tin plum tomatoes Pulped in a blender or mased with a potatoe 
2 x tbls tomatoe puree
2 x tsp italian mixed herbs
1 x tsp oragano
1 x bunch of fresh marajram
1 x oxo chicken stock cube crumbled
1 x oxo vegetable stock cube crumbled
1 x tbls worchester sauce
1 x tsp salt
11/2 x tsp black pepper
400ml water

Method
Heat a large wok temp 6 ( or wide bottomed pan )
Add the oil and as soon as is hot 
Tip - The vegetables need softening not browning as such so temp 5 to 6
Add your onions for 5 mins
Add your carrots for 5 mins
Add your celery and garlic for 5 mins
Add salt and pepper and dried herbs
Once these vegetables have reduced by 2 / 3rds
Push aside and add your bacon lardons to brown for 10 minutes
Push the bacon under the vegetables and start to fry your chicken pieces 
this will take 10 minutes

Tip - Do your chicken in 3 small batches. If you put it all in at once the temperature of the pan will drop and they will boil!
Push your chicken under your vegetables and bacon 

Add your mushrooms stir fry for 5 minutes
Combine all the ingredients in the pan and add your tomatoe pulp puree stock cubes worchester sauce 
Bring to a rapid simmer add water and cover with a well fitting lid turn down to temp 3  for 15 minutes 
Remove the lid turn upto 5 and allow to reduce lastly sprinkle in your marajoram


Top top tip - Your not going to like this idea as your kitchen smells amasing and you just want too eat,
 but until you,ve tried it you will never believe me. Allow the dish to cool competely set it in the fridge over night 
and reheat the next day or even two days later and the taste will have matured and be even better!
