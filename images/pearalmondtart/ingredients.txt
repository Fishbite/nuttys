Ingredients

100g butter
1 tsp cornflour
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 med egg
1 tin pear halves in juice
Save juice for custard!
Icing sugar to dust with

Bake @ 180c 20 to 25mins

Method
In a food mixer, add the first four ingredients
Set to med speed and allow to cream

Grease and line a 15cm cake tin with baking paper

Spread the cake mix into the lined tin
Lay the pear halves on top
Leave some space between the pears

If you have a pizza stone, put it in the oven.
If not, use any earthenware dish and put it in the oven upside down to bake the tart on
