Chorizo Cod Stew

Baked Cod & Chorizo Stew is a gorgeous Spanish dish that is just so wonderfully tasty, really easy to make and completely scrumptious. The unique combination of savory, smokey, spicy flavours of the chorizo along with the aromats of garlic, pepper and paprika permiate through the whole dish making it a complete taste sensation that will please and satisfy any appetite. For a light bite in summer, eat with shreaded lettuce warmed through the sauce and which ever bread you choose or have. On grey days or in the winter time, serve with creamy mashed potatoes to make this a dish of pure indulgent heaven! The starchy, creamy butteriness of the mash really lifts the flavour of the cod and makes the dish delightfully satisfying.

method

As always, chop & prepare everything. Leave your cod in the freezer until you're ready to set the dish into the oven.

Be sure to season each layer of vegetables with a little salt & pepper as you add them to the pan and don't forget to season your fish on both sides! I used a pointy sweet red pepper & cannellini beans but you could use butter beans if you prefer.
